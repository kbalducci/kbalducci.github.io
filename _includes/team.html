My Philosophy
  • Technology is a tool that helps us learn new things, find and evaluate information, share ideas, problem-solve, collaborate and communicate.
  • At the elementary level technology should be integrated into all subjects, but also requires direct instruction so students can use different tech tools effectively.
  • It is important to use technology responsibly. That includes balancing the time spent on technology with physical activity, ensuring that you are not just consuming technology but also using it to create, following cyber safety rules as well as paying attention to your physical self (posture, taking breaks, etc).
